To: Leadership Team

Subject: Analysis of Member Feedback Themes, Severity, and Recommended Operational Improvements
Executive Summary
A review of recent member feedback reveals clear patterns across five major categories: Customer Support, App Issues, Lab Delays, Positive Feedback, and Other Operational Friction. The most urgent issues center on Customer Support responsiveness and App/Scheduling reliability, both of which carry consistently high severity scores (4–5). These issues directly impact member trust, appointment completion, and overall satisfaction.
Despite these pain points, members consistently praise the scan experience, staff professionalism, and clarity of results, indicating that the core clinical service is strong. Operational improvements should therefore focus on communication, scheduling reliability, and proactive updates rather than the scan experience itself.

Key Themes & Insights
1. Customer Support Delays Are the Most Severe Issue
Members report:
No response after multiple attempts
Automated emails with no follow‑up
Missed rescheduling requests
Needing to chase answers
These issues create frustration and erode trust. Severity scores are consistently 4–5, indicating a high operational risk.
2. App & Scheduling Issues Cause Missed Appointments
Members describe:
Booking errors
Confirmations not saving
App showing availability that isn’t real
Appointments disappearing from the system
These failures lead to:
Wasted time
Missed scans
Loss of confidence in the platform
Severity is again 4–5, signaling urgent need for technical remediation.
3. Positive Feedback Highlights Strong Core Experience
Members consistently praise:
Staff professionalism
Comfort during the scan
Clean facilities
Clear results explanations
This indicates that the clinical and in‑person experience is a major strength.
4. Lab Delays Are Low‑Frequency but Noticeable
Members mention:
Wanting faster results
Slightly longer turnaround than expected
Severity is low (2), but expectations could be better managed.
5. Minor Operational Friction Exists
Examples include:
Difficulty finding the facility
Confusing directions
These issues are low severity but easy to fix.
Recommendations
1. Strengthen Customer Support SLAs
Implement a guaranteed 24–48 hour response window
Add automated status updates for open tickets
Introduce triage for high‑severity issues
Track response times as a core operational KPI
Impact: Immediate improvement in member trust and satisfaction.
2. Improve Scheduling & App Reliability
Audit booking logic to prevent false availability
Add confirmation integrity checks
Implement real‑time sync between app and scheduling backend
Add error‑state messaging that guides members to next steps
Impact: Fewer missed appointments and reduced frustration.
3. Proactive Communication for Lab Delays
Automated alerts when results exceed expected turnaround
Clear expectations set during the appointment
Add a “results status” tracker in the app
Impact: Reduces uncertainty and improves transparency.
4. Enhance Wayfinding & Facility Instructions
Add clearer directions in confirmation emails
Include parking, entrance, and floor details
Provide a map link with pinned entrance
Impact: Reduces minor friction and improves first‑time experience.
Conclusion
The core scan experience is strong and consistently praised. The highest‑severity issues stem from communication delays and scheduling system reliability, both of which directly impact member trust and operational efficiency. Addressing these areas will significantly improve the overall member experience and reduce preventable frustration.
I recommend prioritizing improvements to support response times and scheduling system stability in the next operational cycle.
